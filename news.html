Debunking fake news stories

Here is the first example of a fake news that went viral and was belived to be factual by many viewers.The headline reads "Women murders roomate for sending too many candy crush requests."This extremely widely shared piece of fake news reported on the murder of a 19 year old candy crush addict.Her constant game requests drove her college roommate to bludgeon her to death with an industrial-sized bag of jellybeans. Clearly fake, but with 438,599 Facebook engagements, some readers bought it.As told in the stories, there are no credible reports of any such murder to have taken place. The claims actually originated from an 8 March 2016 article on The Valley Report website @thevalleryreport.com
